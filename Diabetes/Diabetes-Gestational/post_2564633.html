COMMUNITY LEADER
Super_sally888
Hi,

it is possible to have gestational diabetes, even if one has no family history.

However, there is an alternative to the test.  You instead get a home blood sugar test meter and test fasting blood sugar (should be in the 70 - 95 range), and two hours post eating (should be < 120).

If you do this testing for several days, record and show to your health care provider, they should accept this in place of the glucose tolerance test.

You can then test from time to time as your pregnancy progresses.
